Classic Rose Cold Cream

Ingredients:

  • Almond oil: 200 milliliters
  • Rose water: 100 milliliters
  • Beeswax: 25 grams
  • Cocoa butter: 25 grams
  • Xanthan gum: 1 gram
  • Rose essential oil: 2 grams

Instructions:

  • Melt the beeswax and cocoa butter gently over low heat.
  • Add the almond oil to the melted mixture.
  • Dissolve the xanthan gum in the rose water.
  • Combine the xanthan gum-rose water mixture with the melted oils.
  • Stir until the mixture cools and thickens.
  • Add the rose essential oil and mix well.

Why You'll Love It: Experience the timeless elegance of Classic Rose Cold Cream. This luxurious blend of almond oil, rose water, beeswax, and cocoa butter offers deep hydration and a soothing touch. Cold cream has been a staple in skincare routines for centuries, dating back to ancient Greece. In the 1890s, it was popular for its moisturizing and soothing properties, often used to cleanse the skin and remove makeup. The inclusion of rose water and almond oil reflects the era's preference for natural, fragrant ingredients that were believed to enhance beauty and provide a luxurious experience. The gentle aroma of rose essential oil adds a refined finish to your skincare routine. Embrace the art of self-care with this natural, handcrafted cream.

Lavender Essence Mist

Ingredients:

  • Ethanol (vodka can be used): 500 milliliters
  • Distilled water: 250 milliliters
  • Lavender essential oil: 10 grams
  • Bergamot essential oil: 5 grams
  • Rosemary essential oil: 2 grams

Instructions:

  • Mix the ethanol and distilled water in a glass container.
  • Add the lavender, bergamot, and rosemary essential oils.
  • Shake well and let the mixture sit for at least 24 hours.
  • Strain the mixture through a fine cloth or coffee filter.
  • Transfer the lavender water to a spray bottle for easy use.

Why You'll Love It: Lavender Essence Mist offers a refreshing and calming experience. This elegant blend of lavender, bergamot, and rosemary essential oils creates a sophisticated fragrance perfect for your body, linens, or as a gentle room spray. Lavender water was a popular fragrance in the 1890s, known for its refreshing and calming properties. It was often used as a body spray, linen spray, or even a mild disinfectant. The combination of lavender, bergamot, and rosemary essential oils not only provided a pleasant scent but also had therapeutic benefits, promoting relaxation and well-being. Enjoy the tranquility and luxury of this handcrafted mist.

Rose Tint Lip Balm

Ingredients:

  • Beeswax: 20 grams
  • Shea butter: 20 grams
  • Almond oil: 30 milliliters
  • Rose essential oil: 1 gram
  • Beetroot powder (for color): 1 gram

Instructions:

  • Melt the beeswax and shea butter gently over low heat.
  • Add the almond oil to the melted mixture.
  • Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  • Stir in the rose essential oil and beetroot powder.
  • Pour the mixture into small containers and let it solidify.

Why You'll Love It: Rose Tint Lip Balm combines the nourishing properties of beeswax, shea butter, and almond oil with the subtle elegance of rose essential oil. Lip salves were essential in the 1890s for protecting and moisturizing the lips, especially during harsh weather conditions. The use of natural ingredients like beeswax, shea butter, and almond oil provided nourishment and protection. Adding a touch of color with beetroot powder was a clever way to enhance the lips' natural beauty, reflecting the era's subtle approach to makeup. The natural tint from beetroot powder adds a delicate touch of color. This handcrafted balm offers a refined way to care for and enhance your lips.
